Privacy Policy

Using the app requires signing in with Apple, which either stores an anonymous email or a unobscured email depending on your choice.

We rely on Firebase to handle authentication as well as store your bird "life list".

The information you store is not discoverable in any way by other users of the app.

Information Collected

  • The birding "life list"
